About Me
Designing Is My Passion

I am fascinated by the artistic elements expressed throughout the spectrum of design and have taken interest in the characterization of digital images. What I find compelling about design is that it enables designers to challenge society's attitudes/norms and it allows the voices of the silenced, oppressed and the marginalized to speak and echo through time. Design generally plays a crucial role in the modern competitive business world. It is more than just aesthetics. Through the manipulation of typography, images, shapes and colors, one is bestowed with the mightiest of abilities; the ability to ignite curiosity, attract, inform, educate, convey complicated information in a digestible way through infographics, develop an identity for businesses through branding, among other things.
